Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my shipment in real-time? expand_more
You can track your shipment by visiting the "My Bookings" page and clicking on the "Track Live Position" button for any ongoing trip.
How is the total trip cost calculated? expand_more
The total cost is the sum of the base freight charges and any toll taxes encountered during the journey. This matches the pattern shown in your digital tax invoice.
Can I contact the driver directly? expand_more
Yes, once a driver accepts your trip, a "Call Driver" button will appear in your booking details on the dashboard.
