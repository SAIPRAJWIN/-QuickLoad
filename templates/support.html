Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid for my trips? add

Payments for all completed trips are processed weekly. The total amount, after deducting our standard commission, is transferred directly to the bank account you've registered with us. You can view all your payouts in the "My Earnings" section.

What happens if a customer cancels a trip? add

Our cancellation policy protects our driver partners. If a customer cancels after you have already started moving towards the pickup location, a standard cancellation fee will be credited to your account to compensate for your time and fuel.

How can I add another vehicle to my profile? add

You can add a new vehicle from the "My Vehicles" page. Click the "Add New Vehicle" button and follow the on-screen instructions to submit the required documents and details. The new vehicle will need to go through our standard verification process.
